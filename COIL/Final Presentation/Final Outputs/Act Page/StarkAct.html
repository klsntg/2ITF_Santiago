Act


Help us act now by helping these Filipino, Cambodian, and International Non-Government Organizations that supports the academic needs of children around the world.

To get more information about these organizations' goals, projects, programs, and events, access their official websites or check their contact details below.


Teach for the Philippines

Teach for the Philippines is a for-purpose, non-stock, non-profit organization that works to ensure all Filipino children benefit from an excellent, inclusive, and relevant education.


Education Foundation of the Philippines

Education Foundation of the Philippines supports schools and other non-profit programs in the Philippines to help provide optimal education for students of the Philippines.


TORM Philippines Education Foundation, Inc.

The TORM Philippines Education Foundation, inc. (TPEF) is a non-stock, non-profit organization established and registered in the Securities & Exchange Commission (SEC) on November 12, 2007. It promotes access to and quality education by granting college scholarships to children of poor families across the country as well as to deserving beneficiaries of Torm Shipping Philippines’ ordinary seafarers and land-based staff and their beneficiaries.



Yours Humanly

Yours Humanly is a global organization dedicated to helping children in need receive the education they deserve. Through our work, we are lifting children out of poverty, trafficking, child labor, and other challenging situations. While the programs and resources we provide vary among the countries and cultures in which we work, our focus is always on transforming the lives of underprivileged children through education.


World Education Cambodia

World Education Cambodia is a field office of World Education, Inc., a nonprofit organization based in Boston, Massachusetts, USA. World Education works with the Cambodian Ministry of Education, Youth and Sport, in addition to other local stakeholders, to develop education in formal and non-formal settings.


Khmer NGO for Education

KHEN is a local NGO and certified Child Safe Organisation based in Battambang Province, Cambodia. We work to promote child rights, accessible, inclusive & quality education and protection of vulnerable children, so that they can develop to their full potential.
