About Us


STARK is an NGO that advocates for children's education in third-world countries. With connections in 20 countries around Asia, STARK has sparked a movement within educational industry, to make education accessible for all, not just to those who can afford it.

STARK is based in the Philippines and Cambodia and started out as an initiative for the Collaborative Online International Learning (COIL) Program in with the joint effort of students from the Royal and Pontifical University of Santo Tomas and Phom Phen International University. The founders were grouped randomly grouped together and decided to form a Non-Government Organization that focuses on the education of children.


Vision

We envision a world where all children are empowered to learn and achieve and have access to high-quality teaching that will equip them with knowledge, abilities, and dispositions for lifelong learning and to contribute to the development of the society.

Mission

To protect and ensure the right of every children to inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all.
